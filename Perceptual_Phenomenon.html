1. What is our independent variable? What is our dependent variable?¶

INDEPENDENT - Condition of Words (Congruent or Incongruent)

DEPENDENT - Response Time(Seconds)

2. What is an appropriate set of hypotheses for this task? What kind of statistical test do you expect to perform? Justify your choices.¶

Ho - Null Hypothesis: There is no significant difference in the population average response time in viewing the congruent(c) words vs viewing the incongruent(i) words.

Ha - Alternative Hypothesis: ( i c ) There is a significant difference,between the population average response times.

The Dependent Samples t-Test is the appropriate statistical test as the same samples are assigned two different conditions. Reason for using t-Test is that that Once we have performed a test in one sample, you might an unfair advantage due to learning effect in doing the same kind of test again on ths same samples.. In addition, we don't have any population parameters provided (so a z-test or f test would not be appropriate here).
